Who Are We?

We are an organisation created with the sole purpose of making literature more understandable and accessible for everyone.

Since starting from nothing we have come a long way while working with charities and sponsors to form partnerships in many different areas.

We do this to ensure our continual growth, expand our reach wherever we can and make it happen for anyone who loves books as much as we do.

We also aim to make the joy of reading available to all while making it easier for those who are disadvantaged and people with disabilities.
